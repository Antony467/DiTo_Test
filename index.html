Priya's voucher pays her back.

Priya finds a physical gift voucher for a spa that expires next week. Instead of letting it go to waste, she uses DiTo.

  • 1

    Scan with Tiny AI & Hash

    Using her phone, DiTo's Tiny AI engine scans the voucher, identifying unique physical traits like texture and print imperfections. This creates a digital fingerprint, which is securely hashed using BLAKE2.

  • 2

    List & Monetize Instantly

    The hashed voucher is listed as an NFT. Even if it doesn't sell and expires, Priya's commitment is rewarded. She starts earning DiToM tokens immediately.

  • 3

    Secure Verification

    When a buyer receives the physical card, they perform the same scan. The hashes must match to finalize the sale, ensuring platform safety and preventing fraud.

Aarav's car, tokenized as an RWA-NFT on DiTo.

Aarav sells his car, earns extra.

Aarav in Chennai lists his car. Priya in Mumbai agrees to buy it for 5 ETH. The physical transfer and paperwork will take 15-30 days.

  • 1

    Payment in Escrow

    Priya pays 5 ETH into DiTo's secure smart contract escrow.

  • 2

    Money Never Sleeps

    While the car is shipped, the 5 ETH is automatically staked in a high-yield Balancer LST pool, generating returns.

  • 3

    Profit on Arrival

    When Priya confirms she received the car, the escrow releases the funds. Aarav gets his 5 ETH plus the yield earned during transit.
